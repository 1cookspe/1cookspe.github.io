Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 3, 2018

String Chooser would like to thank you for using our service to find the perfect tennis strings for your game. String Chooser's primary and sole purpose is to determine the optimal tennis strings that would complement your playing style.

What Information String Chooser Collects and Why

At most, String Chooser collects basic information from your website browser through Google Analytics about your general behavior on the site, including the country you are browsing from, the website that referred you to stringchooser.com, and the general time of your visit. This information is non-invasive, and is only collected to better understand the demographics and preferences of the users of String Chooser. These findings are also basic, and are in no way tied to you as a person; they are only displayed in a broad report that describes the types of users on stringchooser.com. Google Analytics is deployed on most websites simply for the purpose of gauging the general sector of String Chooser's users, to build a better experience to fit their needs or tendencies. For more information on Google's policy regarding their services, please check out HOW GOOGLE USES INFORMATION FROM SITES OR APPS THAT USE OUR SERVICES.

What Information String Chooser Does NOT Collect

String Chooser does NOT collect any sensitive, personal data about you, such as your name, address, phone number, or email address. String Chooser has no intention of trying to collect or steal information from you for the company's own personal gain. Simply put, the only information that String Chooser collects from you is a general idea of your tennis playing style, obtained from the 7 survey questions, which is only computed once to determine optimal tennis strings for your performance, and is erased once the string results are displayed.
