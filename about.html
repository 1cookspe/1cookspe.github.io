About String Chooser

How To Use String Chooser?

Answer 7 quick questions about your playing style, and String Chooser will determine and display the three most suitable strings to complement your game! It's that easy! Click "Choose Your Strings!" at the top of the screen to get started!

Style to Strings

String Chooser is dedicated to providing you with accurate string suggestions that maximize your potential as a tennis player. Through your answers to several questions about your tennis playing style, String Chooser allocates points in numerous qualities of tennis strings, including spin, power, and control. Once all questions are complete, String Chooser then weighs each category against one another, which determines what type of string would most likely fit your needs.

String Chooser sorts tennis strings based on the following characteristics:

  • Spin
  • Power
  • Control
  • Touch
  • Longevity
  • Comfort

From your answers to the survey questions, String Chooser can conclude which of these characterisitcs are most important to you in being a successful tennis player! From there, the best tennis strings that appeal to your style of play will be displayed, pointing you in the right direction for taking your tennis strings to a new level!
