About String Chooser

How To Use String Chooser?

Answer 7 quick questions about your playing style, and String Chooser will determine and display the three most suitable strings to complement your game! It's that easy! Click "Choose Your Strings!" at the top of the screen to get started!

Style to Strings

String Chooser is dedicated to providing you with accurate string suggestions that maximize your potential as a tennis player. Through your answers to several questions about your tennis playing style, String Chooser allocates points in numerous qualities of tennis strings, including spin, power, and control. Once all questions are complete, String Chooser then weighs each category against one another, which determines what type of string would most likely fit the user's needs.
